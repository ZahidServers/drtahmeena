About Dr. Tahmeena

Dr. Tahmeena attended  M.A Rangoonwala College of Dental Sciences and Research Centre in BDS and is a graduate of Maharashtra University of Health Sciences. She futhered her education with many diploma courses. She had previously worked in Masina Hospital and she runned many clinics. She has also worked for Muslim Ambulance Trust. She is now running her independent Clinic.
